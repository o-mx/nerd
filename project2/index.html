GRANNY'S TRIBUTARY

The lady who greately influenced my character

This is my grandma's life;

  • 1975- Born in the serene highlands of Nyeri Constituency, Kenya, into a close-knit and humble family. From an early age, she exhibited remarkable resilience and an innate sense of compassion.
  • 1990- As a young woman, she embraced farming as her calling. Her dedication to the land made her a proficient farmer, mastering the cultivation of crops and livestock to sustain her family. Her humor and warmth began to emerge as defining traits during this time.
  • 1994- Married and started a family of her own. As a mother and caregiver, she became the "glue" of the family, ensuring that love and togetherness thrived in the home.
  • 2000- She deepened her faith by becoming a devoted member of Catholic church community. She remained in this church in all of her lifetime.
  • 2010- Continued to inspire the family with her strength and generosity. Despite the challenges of aging, she remained hardworking and actively involved in farming and family affairs. she became the "glue" of the family, ensuring that love and togetherness thrived in the home.
  • May,2022- Passed away peacefully, leaving behind a legacy of love, hard work, and faith. Her memory lives on in every life she touched—a true testament to a life well-lived.

Granny always said:Life is like tending a farm—nurture what matters, laugh at the weeds, and always share your harvest with love.
